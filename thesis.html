This project developed a rapid, sensitive, and low-cost LAMP assay for detecting E. coli O157:H7 using full colorimetric visualization. The optimized assay achieves reliable amplification within 40 minutes, is equipment-minimal, and provides naked-eye readout suitable for low-resource or point-of-care settings.
